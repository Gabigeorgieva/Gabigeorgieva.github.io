Topic 1: Gabi's Portfolio Page

  1. What is the topic?

    The portfolio page is vital for a portfolio website because without it the website would not be serving its purpose. The portfolio page would allow the viewer to pick from my 4 main areas of my portfolio; Advertising, Logos, Art and UX Design. The purpose to the page would be to organize the sections of my portfolio because depending on the job different recruiters would want to look at different things most relvant to the position they are hiring for. The viewer would then pick which section they would like to look at of my portfolio. The click would then link the viewer to the page that shows the portfolio pieces related to the chosen area.

  2. How topic relates to website idea

    My website idea is to make a portfolio of all my design work to show to future employers. Having a portfolio page would serve the main purpose of a portfolio website. It directs the viewer to what porfolio pieces they wish to look at.

  3. Content displayed?

    The content on this page particularly would be four seperate images that represent the four sections of my portfolio. The image would also be labeled with what section it is with a link attached to the text to take the viewer to that section page that shows the portfolio pieces related to that section.

  4. Example

    An example of this layout can be seen on Josh Miller's Work Page on his portfolio website.
